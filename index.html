Our Teas & C's

Born out of quiet rebellion against the everyday rush, we invite you to slow down. Teas & C's was crafted as a haven for those who believe that life's best moments are unhurried.

We are not a tea room that overlooks coffee, nor a coffee shop that treats tea as an afterthought. We are the meeting point between the two - where both are poured with equal care.

So sip slow, and savour.
